About Us

Our Mission

At this time when cases covid-19 are increasing day by day at a high rate, our mission is to help more and more people without risking their health in danger. As our government suggested us to get quarantined in our home and avoid more and more public contact many of the people are not able to earn for their livelihoods and many are not able to buy goods for their daily use. so, keeping this pandemic in our mind and to compensate these type of critical problems which many of the indians are facing right now to help more and more people. there are separate options for each and every individual you can register as a customer, as a student, as a worker or you can apply for new jobs on the basis of your skills. This website also requires access to your aarogya setu app, and it is necessary that you have that app in your phone. in this way we also promote our country and also the indian developers of that app. while registering for this website you have to mention your skills or needs. suppose you want a plumber than enter your need and address than automatically this website will enlist all the plumbers available in your area. In that person's details you can see his aarogya setu details in this way you can know that from which zone (red zone, green zone, orange zone) is he coming from and whether he is safe from covid 19 or not. The same procedure goes for the worker. For the students who are not able to study due to the shortage of study materials we will provide online courses and internships. they have to mention their field , branch and course they would like. for the people who unfortunately become jobless in this pandemic they can upload tutorials , courses and can teach students online in form to earn .

Our Vision

As our country is in the top 5 of most corona patients with 410k confirmed cases and 13,260 deaths. Our primary vision is to control this pandemic as much as we can. and our second vision behind this to make India Atma Nirbhar(self dependent). as we promote the Aarogya Setu app and encourage people to not lose hope and keep working . We want to improve the leadership quality of the students and also want to provide the workers who don't have any jobs right now . we promote self-employment and aatmnirbharta.in this way people will buy and use the products that are made in india, we can boycott any country if we want. We also provide more and more courses to students from many experienced people on this website. We promote emotions more than rational people.
